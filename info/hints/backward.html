Let make some simple test. What will be, if you open your python console and say "a = [1, 2, 3]" and than use a negative step to this list?

What is "negative step"?

You can use a negative step to obtain a reversed list:

a = [1, 2, 3]
a[::-1]

After this you will get a reversed list [3, 2, 1]
